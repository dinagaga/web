A Team of Web3 Experts

Blockjoy started as a project to manage staking with friends and family.

We built advanced tooling to optimize validator management for teams of two. But, due to the sheer difficulty of installing and running nodes, our little Vodi company grew crazy fast. We quickly realized that Vlad's can't work for a network because it centralizes control. We turned our platform into a SaaS tool so that anyone could launch and run their own node with full control over where and on what infrastructure it runs. Now, network operators can run their own nodes. Blockjoy just takes the headaches away.
